## Lens Color Matrix

| Color | Best For | DuberyMNL Fit |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Gray | All-around, all light conditions | Strong general everyday use |
| Amber | Sight fishing, low light, general outdoor | Everyday outdoor, max brightness field of view |
| Copper | Sight fishing, general on/off water, contrast | Casual outdoor, versatile |
| Silver Mirror | Variable light, freshwater sight fishing | Mid-range versatile |
| Green Mirror | Inshore/flats saltwater, freshwater rivers | Combo premium lens angle |
| Blue Mirror | Clear water, extreme bright sun, beach | Strong PH year-round fit (tropical sun) |
| Sunrise/Yellow | Dawn/dusk only, boat driving, safety glasses | Specialty/niche only |

## Key Rules
- **Polarization is #1 buying criterion** for active outdoor users — more important than color
- Blue Mirror = best PH fit — tropical sun is effectively year-round extreme brightness
- Amber = highest everyday versatility for outdoor workers (fishermen, commuters, construction)
- Green Mirror + Silver Mirror only in infographic, not article text

## Content Hooks
- **Polarization test video** (high shareability): hold lens horizontal to phone/computer screen → visible; rotate vertical → goes black if polarized. Simple demo, low effort, builds trust.
- **Lens color guide caption**: "Hindi lahat ng sunnies polarized" — educational angle that differentiates DuberyMNL from cheap non-polarized alternatives
- **PH use case framing**: Gray = commute, Blue Mirror = beach/fishing trip, Amber = early morning outdoor work

**How to apply:** When writing captions or planning educational content about DuberyMNL sunglasses — use lens color positioning to speak to specific outdoor activities rather than generic UV protection claims.
