## POSITIONING CONTRADICTION -- 2026-04-11 (Jordan Platten ingest)

Jordan Platten's "Top 3 AI Systems" video (youtu.be/KqjWm2bexUc) directly challenges the portfolio positioning (NOT the tool learning):

- Jordan calls Make/Zapier/n8n work **"petty automation"** — says it has no measurable revenue outcome, clients churn in <30 days, and business owners now build these themselves with ChatGPT.
- The three systems that actually sell: **AI Funnel Machine** (ads→landing→AI appointment setter), **Automated Outbound Engine** (multichannel cold outreach with AI), **Creative Goldmine** (AI-generated ad creative for paid accounts).
- Retainer ranges: $2K-$10K/mo starter, $10K-$50K+ bundled, $50K-$100K for full-stack acquisition departments.

**Impact on this plan:**
- **Keep** the tool learning (Make/Zapier/n8n are still useful internal skills for building the systems Jordan describes).
- **Change** the public positioning. Portfolio should NOT lead with "automation builder" or generic workflow demos. Lead with **"AI Lead Generation Systems for E-commerce"** or similar revenue-focused framing.
- **Reframe DuberyMNL** as the live case study for System 1 (Messenger funnel + chatbot) + System 3 (UGC/brand content pipeline). That bundle is Jordan's $10K+/mo tier.
- **Reprice RAS AI SOLUTIONS** — `work.md` currently says "$1K-$2.5K end-to-end." Jordan's data suggests $1.5K-$3K/mo retainer is the floor. Consider switching from project fees to monthly retainers.
- **GHL trial timing still valid** — Jordan's logic reinforces: don't sign up until a client is lined up. Tool demos alone don't sell.
