**Unity recipe (the 4 constants — these are what make a varied 7-pack read as one campaign):**
1. Brand lockup pinned top-left or top-right, small, never centered
2. Headline always sits on a brushstroke/ribbon shape, never bare on photo
3. Circle-arrow "swipe next" CTA bottom-center
4. Sunset-orange lens reflection as recurring visual motif across product slides

**6 layout formats (carousel taxonomy):**
HERO_PORTRAIT (ribbon text around face), PRODUCT_HERO (close-up frame + scenic lens reflection), MULTI_PRODUCT_GRID (floating frames + neon feature tags), GROUP_LIFESTYLE (2-3 models in different SKUs), UGC_WALL (4 polaroids on torn-paper backings + hashtag), CLOSING_CTA (product hero + directive headline + URL/email footer).

**Type stack:** condensed chunky sans on brushstroke (headlines) + script/italic one-liner (sub-head quotes) + wide-tracked thin caps (title-card moments). Three roles, three voices.

**Mood wash:** bg color rotates per slide (cyan → green → peach → lilac) — cheaper than locking one palette and reads as curated as long as the 4 constants stay fixed.

**Tone caveat:** Glowwave is LOUDER than DuberyMNL's locked "quiet confidence" voice (`project_dubery_v2_brand_identity.md`). Take the structure and constants, not the "!" copy or over-saturated palette.
