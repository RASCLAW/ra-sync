Remove all dark/evening/night scenes from the variety banks. Sunglasses are a daytime product -- night market, neon lighting, blue-hour scenes don't make sense for the brand.

**Why:** R2 test (session 119) looked good visually but RA flagged it as not applicable. "Who wears sunglasses at night?"

**How to apply:** When building scene variety banks or randomizers, exclude: night markets, neon lighting, blue-hour twilight, evening settings. Stick to daytime scenes -- golden hour is the latest acceptable lighting.
