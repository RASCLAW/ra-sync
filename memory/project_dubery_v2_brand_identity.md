**Identity:** DuberyMNL is for outdoor people who appreciate seeing the world clearly — polarization as revelation, not just sun protection.

**Hero line:** "Dubery. Made for the view."
**Sub:** "Polarized sunglasses built for the outdoor life."

**Section copy (locked):**
- 001 / Clarity — "Polarized lenses change what you see." Body hints at the science: physically blocks light waves, doesn't explain specs.
- 002 / Collections — Series names only. Bandits / Outback / Rasta. No descriptions.
- 003 / Community — "Shop our Facebook." Facebook community social proof.
- 004 / Best Sellers — "This Month's Favorites." Product names + price.
- 005 / Value — "Scratch-proof. Polarized. Better price." Body: brands charging 10x, no flagship stores, no celebrity sponsors.
- 006 / CTA — "Grab a pair now." Body: next-day delivery, COD Metro Manila, ships nationwide.

**Why:** RA's customer = beach-goers and outdoor people who appreciate the world better through a polarized lens. Not aspirational lifestyle marketing — the product itself changes the experience.

**How to apply:** Don't drift back to generic DTC copy ("UV400 blocks harmful rays"). Always anchor to the experience of seeing clearly, not the spec. Product name + what it does to your view.
