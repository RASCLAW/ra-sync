**Glossy finish:** Bandits Glossy Black, Bandits Blue, Bandits Green
**Matte finish:** Everything else (Bandits Matte Black, Bandits Tortoise, all Outbacks, all Rastas)

Only 3 out of 11 variants are glossy. This matters for image generation prompts -- material finish affects how the model renders surface reflections and texture.
