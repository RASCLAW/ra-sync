**Locked 2026-04-11** after Jordan Platten ingest + strategic discussion with RA.

## Brand Name

**RAS Creative SOLUTIONS** (renamed from "RAS AI SOLUTIONS")

**Why the rename:** "Creative" frames the outcome; "AI" is just the how. AI is a crowded positioning — "Creative Solutions" positions RA as a creative/outcomes partner, not another AI vendor. Use "RAS Creative SOLUTIONS" in all client-facing copy.

## Niche

**Always-On Acquisition Systems for Service Businesses.**

Target verticals (broad positioning): dental clinics, med spas, aesthetics, real estate brokers, law firms, home services (aircon, cleaning, pest), gyms, personal trainers, coaches, wedding/event photographers, tutoring.

**Actual prioritized niche list (see `project_ras_creative_niches.md` for the strategic narrowing):** solar panel installers (primary) → battery storage (paired) → tour operators → wedding photographers → real estate → immigration consultants → car detailing. Dental/spa and review centers explicitly dropped.

**Why services over products:**
- Higher margins justify higher CPAs
- Trust-building fits Messenger/DM culture (especially PH "magkano?" flow)
- Appointment-based flow is natural
- Repeat customers + referrals built in
- Service businesses genuinely need funnels (unlike e-commerce that falls back on marketplaces)

## Positioning Statement (VERBATIM — do not rewrite)

> Service businesses lose leads at every stage of the funnel. At the top, winning ad creatives are too expensive and burns out quickly to keep up. In the middle, replies arrive late, emails feel generic, inboxes are chaotic, forgotten follow-ups, and the team buried under everything else. At the bottom, customers book with almost no context, scheduling becomes a back-and-forth mess, no follow up reminders, and abandoned bookings die silently.
>
> We close every one of those gaps with always-on acquisition systems: fresh ads creative that never runs out, instant personalized replies, automatic lead scoring and follow-up, and bookings that come in with full context plus reminders that actually land.
>
> We're RAS Creative SOLUTIONS — we build the systems that makes a small business run like a 12-person marketing department end to end.

**Rules for using this copy:**
- Do NOT add workflow details (Messenger, chatbot, landing page) to the positioning statement
- Do NOT mention background/credentials in the positioning statement itself
- Use it verbatim in: portfolio hero, proposal cover, cold outreach, LinkedIn About, Upwork headline

## The Moat

RA's years at Google's worldwide fallout leads team (via TDCX BPO). He manually qualified 200+ stalled Google Ads registrations per day — businesses that filled forms but didn't complete, free trial users who didn't convert, high-intent signals that went cold. At Google TOS quality standards, worldwide.

**Why this matters:** Most AI builders guess what a qualified lead looks like. RA doesn't — he spent years deciding, one website at a time, at global scale. His AI qualification layer will work where others produce garbage because the scoring logic comes from real manual experience.

## Offer Components (Internal — not in client copy)

1. **Creative engine** — AI-generated ads, daily posts, UGC clips, replenished weekly, no burnout
2. **Entry layer** — landing pages + Messenger/DM funnels (client's choice or both)
3. **Lead capture** — form webhooks + chatbot, unified queue to CRM
4. **AI qualification layer** — HOT/WARM/COLD scoring + routing (the unique IP)
5. **Nurture** — email drips, DM follow-ups, SMS reminders
6. **Booking** — AI appointment setter + calendar integration + reminders + abandoned-booking rescue
7. **Reporting** — weekly dashboard: spend, leads, bookings, CPL, LTV

## Pricing (Retainer — NOT one-time)

- **Starter:** P30K-P50K/mo (PH) or $1.5K-$3K/mo (international) — full funnel + 4-8 creatives/mo
- **Bundled:** $3K-$7K/mo — funnel + creative + ad management
- **Premium:** $7K-$15K/mo — full acquisition department with case studies + performance fees

## Outbound Strategy

Targeted, small-scale (20-50 high-quality leads/day), personalized per-business. Not 3K emails/day. Goal is case study clients, not volume. This matches RA's bandwidth (night shift + baby) and aligns with his Google muscle memory (qualification > volume).

## Secondary Revenue — Ascension Path

Once embedded via lead gen, expand into ops automation, internal dashboards, AI operating systems. This is where retainer goes from $3K → $10K+/mo. (Jordan Platten's "infrastructure tier" / Ascension Path.)
