RedFlash PH is a fictional Manila-based tattoo illustrator created for DuberyMNL brand storytelling. First used in a caption for the Rasta Red tattoo art image (2026-05-19).

**Why:** Gives bespoke/illustrated content a story, makes posts feel like organic fan art or collabs, builds brand lore over time without paid influencer costs.

**How to apply:** Reference @redflash.ph in captions for illustrated/bespoke art content. Keep persona consistent — quiet, lets the work speak, never self-promotes.

## Identity

- **IG Handle:** @redflash.ph
- **Style:** Neo-traditional tattoo illustration — heavy botanicals, warm palettes, bold linework, flash sheets
- **Location:** Manila-based
- **Personality:** Low-profile. No face photos. Just drops work. Dubery reposts, he never asks for it.
- **Backstory:** Been in the local tattoo scene for years. Discovered Dubery through a friend, bought a Rasta Red, and the art came naturally. Sent it to Dubery DMs unsolicited.
