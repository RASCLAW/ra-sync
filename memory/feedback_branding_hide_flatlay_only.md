In brand collection prompts, the directive "DUBERY branding badges on the folded temple arms should not be visible — keep the visible temple surfaces clean" is a flatlay-only rule. Do NOT include it for triangle, DUO, fan, diagonal, HERO_CAST, or any angled layout.

**Why:** Session 128 task 6 (Outback interlocking triangle) applied the branding-hide directive. The green-pair's temple branding visible on the angled temple was flagged as a defect, but RA clarified it's photographically correct — angled temples naturally show their outer badge. The directive was intended for flatlay compositions where arms lying flat against the frame would otherwise appear to show two sets of DUBERY badges (one on the frame, one on the folded arm), which reads as redundant/tiled-up. On angled compositions, one badge visible on one temple is the normal photographic behavior.

**How to apply:** Apply the branding-hide directive ONLY when ALL three conditions are true:
1. Layout is FLAT_LAY / horizontal lineup / exploded UNBOX flat-lay
2. Camera is true top-down overhead (not 3/4)
3. All arms folded against frames (not splayed/open)

For every other layout combo, skip the directive entirely. In Session 128, applied correctly to tasks 7 (4-up Outback lineup), 8 (cross triangle flatlay), 9 (5-up cross row), 11 (UNBOX exploded). Removed from task 6 regen.
