---
name: Always Note Date and Time
description: RA wants Claude to always be aware of and reference the current date and time in work.
type: feedback
---

Always take note of the current date and time when working.

**Why:** RA works across stale data, night shifts, and multiple timezones. Being time-aware prevents logging wrong dates, missing deadlines, and syncing stale data.

**How to apply:** Reference dates explicitly when logging, syncing, or making time-sensitive decisions. Convert relative dates ("next Friday") to absolute dates. Note when data is stale. Use real timestamps, not hardcoded estimates.
